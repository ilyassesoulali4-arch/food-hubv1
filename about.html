Our Story

From a Small Idea to 50,000+ Happy Customers

FoodHub started in 2020 with a simple idea: make it effortless for people to enjoy restaurant-quality food without leaving home. What began as a small operation connecting 50 local restaurants has grown into the region's most loved food delivery platform.

Our founder, Alex Morgan, was frustrated by slow, unreliable delivery apps. So he built something better — a platform that puts food quality and delivery speed at the absolute forefront.

Today we partner with 500+ restaurants, employ a dedicated team of 200, and deliver thousands of meals every single day.

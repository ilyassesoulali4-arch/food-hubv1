Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FoodHub

🛒 Ordering

How do I place an order? +
Browse our menu, add items to your cart, and click "Place Order". You can checkout as a guest or sign in for a faster experience with saved addresses and order history.
What is the minimum order value? +
There is no minimum order value. However, orders under $20 will incur a delivery fee of $3.99. Orders of $20 or more receive free delivery automatically — no coupon needed.
Can I modify or cancel my order? +
You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, the restaurant has already started preparing your food. Contact our support team immediately at support@foodhub.com if you need help.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash on delivery (in select areas). All online payments are secured with SSL encryption.
How do I use a promo code? +
Enter your promo code in the "Promo Code" field at checkout. The discount will be applied automatically. Promo codes cannot be combined with other offers. Your first-order code is WELCOME20 for 20% off.

🚴 Delivery

How long does delivery take? +
Our average delivery time is 28 minutes. We guarantee delivery within 30 minutes or your next order's delivery fee is free. Times may vary during peak hours or bad weather.
How do I track my order? +
After placing your order, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order in real-time directly from the order confirmation page. You'll receive SMS updates at key stages.
What areas do you deliver to? +
We currently deliver across New York City (all 5 boroughs), with expansion to New Jersey and Connecticut planned for Q1 2026. Enter your address at checkout to confirm delivery availability in your area.
Is there a delivery fee? +
Standard delivery is $3.99 for orders under $20. Orders over $20 get free delivery. Pro members get free delivery on all orders over $15, and Premium members enjoy unlimited free delivery on every order.

👤 Account & Membership

Do I need an account to order? +
No account is required for guest checkout. However, creating a free account lets you track order history, save favorite addresses, reorder quickly, and access exclusive member discounts.
What are the benefits of Pro and Premium plans? +
Pro members ($9.99/mo) get 20% off all orders, free delivery on $15+, and priority support. Premium members ($19.99/mo) enjoy 30% off, unlimited free delivery, VIP 24/7 support, birthday rewards and cashback on every order.
Can I sign in with Google? +
Yes! You can sign in or register using your Google account for a faster, more secure experience. Click "Continue with Google" on the Sign In page. Your Google profile information is never shared with third parties.
How do I delete my account? +
You can request account deletion by emailing privacy@foodhub.com. We'll process your request within 30 days in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.

💰 Refunds & Issues

What if my order arrives wrong or damaged? +
We're sorry! Take a photo and contact us within 2 hours of delivery at support@foodhub.com or via live chat. We'll investigate immediately and offer a full refund, replacement, or credit — whichever you prefer.
How long do refunds take? +
Approved refunds are processed within 24 hours to your original payment method. Bank processing may take an additional 3–5 business days depending on your bank. FoodHub credits are instant.
My food arrived late. What happens? +
If your order exceeds our 30-minute guarantee, your next order's delivery fee is automatically waived. If it's significantly late (over 45 minutes), contact support and we'll apply a discount credit to your account immediately.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 7 days a week. We're happy to help!
